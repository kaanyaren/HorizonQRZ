Cloud Sync

Backed by Supabase — your logs are safe in the cloud. Log a QSO on your phone, see it everywhere. Works offline and syncs when you reconnect.

📤

One-Click Upload

Upload QSOs to QRZ.com, Club Log, and eQSL.cc in one tap. No more manual ADIF exports and imports across platforms.

🏆

Contest Ready

Built-in contest mode with auto-incrementing serial numbers, exchange validation, and N1MM-compatible ADIF export.

🔒

Secure Auth

Sign in with Google or Apple. No passwords to remember. Your credentials are encrypted at rest using platform secure storage.

📊

Smart Export

Filter by contest, band, mode, or date range. Export clean ADIF files and share via AirDrop, email, or cloud storage.

🏅

Awards Tracking

Track DXCC, WAS, and other award progress directly in the app with QRZ XML awards integration.
